Privacy

This site uses statnive.live — a privacy-first web-analytics platform. No third-party tracker is loaded; no canvas, WebGL, or font-availability probes run; your raw IP address is never stored.

Your rights

You can object to analytics at any time. Clicking Opt out sets a strictly-necessary _statnive_optout cookie; the server then silently drops every subsequent event from your browser.

Access and erasure requests are served at GET /api/privacy/access and POST /api/privacy/erase respectively. Both require your _statnive cookie to be present so the server can find the rows tied to your browser.
